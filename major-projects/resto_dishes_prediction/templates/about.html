Behind the Logic

Understanding the machine learning architecture powering DishPredict.

The Problem

Restaurants often launch new menu items based on intuition rather than data. This leads to high failure rates and wasted inventory. DishPredict solves this by analyzing historical data to quantify the likelihood of a dish's success before it hits the kitchen.

The Solution

We use a Logistic Regression model trained on specific cuisine datasets. The model evaluates features like:

  • Spiciness levels & Prep time
  • Price-to-value ratios
  • Dietary categorization (Veg/Non-veg)
